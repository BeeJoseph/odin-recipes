Loaded Macaroni and Cheese

Description

The recipe includes using these main flavors: four kinds of cheeses, bacon, scallions, breadcrumbs, and of course, macaroni. It will be sure to leave you filled after just 1 serving.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Bacon
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Scallions
  • Whole milk
  • Sour cream
  • 4 cheese blend
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Salt

Steps

  8. Top the the macaroni with the remaining bacon and cheese, and the toasted breadcrumbs.
