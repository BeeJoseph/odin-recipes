Espresso Brownies

Description

These espresso brownies are the perfect way to wake you up in the morning. Lightly crispy on the outside, and fudgy in the inside, they are made using espresso powder. As opposed to an airy, light, fluffy texture, these brownies have a decadent and chewy texture that will be sure to satisy you with a glass of good ol' milk.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate chips
  • Vanilla
  • Cocoa powder
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Espresso
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a bowl, mix all the dry ingredients together: flour, cocoa powder, espresso, and salt.
  3. On a stove, melt the butter and the chocolate chips in a glass bowl over a steam of boiling water.
    4. In a separate bowl, combine the dry ingredients and the melted chocolate
  4. Add the vanilla and eggs.
  5. Mix.
  6. Pour the mix into a rectangular baking pan.
  7. Bake for 10-11 minutes.
  8. Cut into 16 squares.
  9. Enjoy.
